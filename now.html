Enjoying the weather

As absolutely horrific as the weather tends to be, it can still be enjoyable somtimes.
I've always enjoyed autumn, with the beautiful colors and cooler weather it was
always far more pleasing to me than say, summer--with it's melting temperatures and high humidity.

That's not to say I don't or haven't enjoyed summer, I certainly have. Most days though
are ruined by how miserable the heat becomes. Maybe I'm just sensitive to the heat
but I tend to melt just by standing around in your average Walmart.
